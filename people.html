Anton Korinek

Faculty Director | Professor of Economics & Darden School of Business

Professor Anton Korinek is a leading economist specializing in macroeconomics, financial crises, and the economics of artificial intelligence. As Faculty Director of EconTAI, he leads the initiative's research agenda, focusing on how transformative AI will reshape labor markets, productivity, and economic inequality while developing policy frameworks to ensure broadly shared prosperity.

Research Interests: Economics of Artificial Intelligence, Macroeconomics and Financial Stability, Labor Markets and Automation, Economic Policy and Governance
