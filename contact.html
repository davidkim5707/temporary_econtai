Collaboration Opportunities

For Researchers

We welcome collaboration with scholars across economics, computer science, and related fields. Opportunities include joint research projects, visiting scholar positions, and participation in workshops and conferences.

For Policymakers

EconTAI provides expert consultation on AI economic policy at local, national, and international levels. We offer policy briefings, expert testimony, and collaborative research on policy-relevant questions.

For Organizations

We partner with businesses, foundations, and international organizations on research projects, educational initiatives, and strategic consulting on AI's economic implications.

For Students

Graduate students interested in AI economics can engage with EconTAI through research assistantships, coursework, and participation in our seminars and workshops. Contact us to learn about current opportunities.
