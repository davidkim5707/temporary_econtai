Economics of Transformative AI (EconTAI)

A pioneering research initiative at the University of Virginia dedicated to understanding and shaping the economic implications of transformative artificial intelligence.

We bridge the gap between economic theory and AI governance by producing rigorous research, building field capacity, and informing policy decisions during the transition to an AI-transformed economy.

University of Virginia | Led by Professor Anton Korinek

Our Mission

Vision

A world in which transformative AI expands prosperity and human flourishing.

Learn More →

Mission

To produce and disseminate cutting-edge economic research that helps society navigate the transition to transformative AI, ensuring that advanced AI systems contribute to broadly shared prosperity while mitigating the risks of economic disruption and inequality.

Learn More →

Strategic Objectives

Research Excellence: Produce world-class economic research on transformative AI's implications for labor markets, productivity, inequality, and economic growth
Education and Field Building: Cultivate a community of scholars on TAI at UVA and globally and train the next generation of researchers and practitioners
Policy Translation: Deliver timely research insights that are useful for policymakers at the local, national, and international level
Public Understanding: Enhance public discourse on AI's economic implications through accessible communication and education
Innovation in Methods: Pioneer new research methodologies that develop AI tools for economic research, including to study AI's economic impacts
