Economics of Transformative AI Initiative

EconTAI

A pioneering research initiative at the University of Virginia dedicated to understanding and shaping the economic implications of transformative artificial intelligence. We bridge the gap between economic theory and AI governance by producing rigorous research, building field capacity, and informing policy decisions during the transition to an AI-transformed economy.
