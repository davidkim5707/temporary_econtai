Our Mission & Vision

Shaping the economic future of transformative artificial intelligence

Vision

A world in which transformative AI expands prosperity and human flourishing.

Mission

To produce and disseminate cutting-edge economic research that helps society navigate the transition to transformative AI, ensuring that advanced AI systems contribute to broadly shared prosperity while mitigating the risks of economic disruption and inequality.

Why EconTAI Exists

The Challenge

Transformative artificial intelligence has the potential to fundamentally reshape our economy and society. As AI capabilities advance, we face unprecedented questions about labor markets, wealth distribution, economic growth, and the very nature of human work and prosperity. The economic implications are profound, and the window to shape positive outcomes is limited.

The Gap

While significant attention has been paid to AI's technical capabilities and safety challenges, the economic dimension has been comparatively underexplored. We need rigorous economic research to understand how transformative AI will affect employment, productivity, inequality, and economic institutions‚Äîand to develop policy frameworks that ensure broadly shared benefits.

Our Response

EconTAI bridges this gap by bringing together world-class economic researchers at the University of Virginia to tackle the economic dimensions of transformative AI. We combine rigorous theoretical analysis, empirical research, and policy engagement to generate actionable insights for policymakers, businesses, and society. Our work ensures that economic considerations are central to AI governance and that the transition to an AI-powered economy benefits all of humanity.
