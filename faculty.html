Core Team

Leading scholars and directors advancing the economics of transformative AI

Faculty Director

Anton Korinek

Professor of Economics & Darden School of Business

Professor Anton Korinek is a leading economist specializing in macroeconomics, financial crises, and the economics of artificial intelligence. As Faculty Director of EconTAI, he leads the initiative's research agenda, focusing on how transformative AI will reshape labor markets, productivity, and economic inequality while developing policy frameworks to ensure broadly shared prosperity.

Research Interests

  • Economics of Artificial Intelligence
  • Macroeconomics and Financial Stability
  • Labor Markets and Automation
  • Economic Policy and Governance

Associate Director

Sydney Cullen

Associate Manager & Full-time Director, EconTAI Initiative

Sydney Cullen serves as the Associate Director and full-time operational leader of EconTAI. She manages the initiative's day-to-day operations, coordinates research activities, and facilitates collaboration across faculty, students, and external partners to advance EconTAI's mission of understanding AI's economic implications.

Key Focus Areas

  • Initiative Operations & Management
  • Research Coordination & Support
  • Stakeholder Engagement & Partnerships
  • Event Planning & Community Building
